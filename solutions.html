Our expertise is pretty unique in that it covers a range of activities related to document engineering : web crawling, natural language processing, machine learning or search.

We provide consulting services and custom development using leading open source projects such as Apache Nutch, GATE, SOLR or ElasticSearch.

DigitalPebble's director, Julien Nioche, is a member of the Apache Software Foundation and a long standing committer to Apache Nutch. Julien is a contributor and committer on several open source projects as well as a conference speaker.

Over the years we have actively contributed to some of the projects above and combined them to build bespoke solutions for our customers. We have a strong focus on very large scale processing and have developed solutions based on Hadoop or Storm and deployed them on platforms such as Amazon AWS.

Whether your project requires web crawling, text classification, NLP or indexing, feel free to contact us, we'd be happy to help!
